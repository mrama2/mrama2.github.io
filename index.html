Biography

Ramakrishnan Maharajan is currently an Associate professor in Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, SRM University AP. Previously, he worked as Project Lead, L&T Technology Services. He received his BE degree from University of Madras, and the M.E and PhD degree from College of Engineering, Anna University, He did his PhD with UGC fellowship. After his doctorate, he worked in industry in various positions for Embedded Product development for a decade.

 

Projects

1.    Real Time Blood Pressure Measurement System using Pulse Transit Time.

The project involves the firmware development in a biosensor module for synchronous capturing of ECG and PPG signals which is used to calculate the Pulse Transit Time (PTT). This PTT is used for measuring Systolic Blood Pressure and Diastolic Blood Pressure.

2.    Energy Neutral Adaptive Duty Cycle MAC for Energy Harvesting WSN.

The project involves the development of Energy Neutral Medium Access Control protocol for Solar Energy Harvesting Wireless Sensor Networks (EH-WSN)
